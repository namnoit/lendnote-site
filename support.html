Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All of your financial data — contacts, transactions, and debt records — is stored locally on your device only. It is not uploaded to any cloud service. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How does the Smart Add feature work?

With Smart Add you can type a transaction in plain language (for example, "Lent John $50 for lunch"). The text is sent securely to our AI parsing service, which extracts the amount, person, date, and type. The text is not retained after processing, and no other app data is sent.

How do I delete my data?

Because your data lives only on your device, you can remove individual records inside the app, or delete all data at once by uninstalling Lendnote from your device.
